As the shredding guitar
gives way to the energetic melody,
the husky, earnest voice
shouts at me: *rewrite.*

It's resonant pitch
pins me down,
as the echoed wisdom
pierces my heart.

*リライト*, it repeats.
Not like a command
questioning my skill,
but my grip.

An aspiration that conveys
complete surrender of all ifs.
The raw hint of the choice
to reshape our missteps.

Shatter harmful lies
with every drum strike,
cathartic insights hype,
with each electrifying cry.

Spring back to life
as a leaf craving for light,
give it all you've got;
Love is the only way to *rewrite*.
