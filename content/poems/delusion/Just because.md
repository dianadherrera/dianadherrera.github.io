---
title: Just because
weight: 5
date: 2026-03-21
extra:
  entry_type: poem
---

When young, 
life feels at the expense 
of almost everything.

Habits, whims.
Lifetime decisions
taken as inconsequential.

I took the class 
*just because* I wanted
to walk home with her every day.

We practiced sports 
*just because* we would rather 
not be home.

With such ease, 
we mangle destiny,
worried by trivial thoughts.

Later in life,
all those micro decisions
will lose their thread.

Until we remember,
the power 
we attributed to chance.
