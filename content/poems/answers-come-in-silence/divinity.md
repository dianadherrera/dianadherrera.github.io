No one
should know,
they warn me.

Don't
let them see,
it is ugly.

Light clothes,
fresh smiles,
cool looks.

Flames
exploding with
devouring angst.

Vivid
orange hues
coloring the sky.

Amidst
the haze,
a sacred dance.

A smoky smell,
palms carbonized.

She whispered
_ignite it all._

Ecstatic,
I entered
the blaze.

Refreshed,
as I burned
to ashes.
