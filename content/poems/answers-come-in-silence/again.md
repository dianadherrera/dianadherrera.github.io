Lost and found,
the ultimate game.
Curiosity is the challenge,
we must not forget.

Rather invisible
when you blend with thought.
Spotted abruptly
when you're calm enough.

Bewildered and thankful
whenever I find you.
You pick the strangest places
I wonder, what drive you?

No matter where you are
I will join you, for sure.
just play one more time,
and delight in the joy.

This is my lifetime's game,
I will play it till the end.
