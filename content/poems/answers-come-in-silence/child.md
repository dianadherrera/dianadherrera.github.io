Precious and warm,
gracious and mine.

Pure and untouched
the flames deep inside.

Give birth to the child,
the child of fire.

Let it consume your insides
and kill your desire.

Playful and unstable,
improper and crude.

Loud and nasty,
let them stare in reprove.

Flourish sincerely
willfull and strong,

dynamic and nimble
nature is not wrong.

Embrace your own fire
protect it with pride

Be willing to shed,
the skin that was liked.
