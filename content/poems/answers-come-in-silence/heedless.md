title: Heedless
weight: 11
date: '2024-10-19'

---

A world full of corpses
looking with empty eyes.

A battle for the last word
with whoever wants to try.

Spilling cover letters
walking advertisement.

Talking without presence
rambling in excitement.

Dead are the heedless
the ones without intention.

Speak wisely and be careful
for _Mara_ is always next door.
