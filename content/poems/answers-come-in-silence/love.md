A look into a mirror,
suddenly, perspective
has switched.

From the thirsty desire
of fantasy and storytelling
to a sound reality.

From the restless compulsion
of showing off and pretending
to a confident acknowledge.

From the strenuous striving
of convincing through words
to a quiet knowing.

From the corrosive temptation
of manipulation and insincerity
to a courageous acceptance.

From the scorching intensity
of excitement and anticipation
to the coolness of peace.

From the despairing ideas
of comparison and envy
to a keen appreciation.

From the insatiable void
for praise and uniqueness
to the balm of full presence.

A soothing contentment
suffused the heart,
after dew drops overflowed
the dam of delusion.
