A kind act on the street
not as easy as it sounds.
You have to look around
forget about the time.

Have a mind of service
what is it that you need?
Let me be the honored one
that fulfill the deed.

Hearts intertwined
if only for seconds.
Enough to leave a mark
that may last for milleniums.

Sweet little actions
a rare kind of treasure,
they water the seeds
that will bring us protection.
