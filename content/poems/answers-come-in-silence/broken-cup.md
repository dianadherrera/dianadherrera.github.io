---
title: Broken cup
weight: 6
date: '2024-10-19'
extra:
  entry_type: poem
---

Death and life,
may we share it all.
No preference, no resistance.

Compassion and despair,
gratitude and sorrow,
laughs and sighs.

Each moment is precious,
so with full presence,
I venerate it.

My cup is now full.
An infusion of your love
warms my heart.
