Memories of a present past

Come and show me the way

I want to learn about myself

The future and now

There is nothing i cannot get

So be bountiful with your advice

No one by My SIDE

That will push me away

Let the knowledge flow

As rivers of blood

Tell me the signs

I often forget

Be cautious with water

The one that brings hate

Remenber your calling 

And you will never fail

Notice your surroundings

The ones that tap on your shoulders

Be the one who transmit

Their souls Journey
