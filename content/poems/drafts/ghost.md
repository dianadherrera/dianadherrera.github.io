Translucent skin, pale bones.
Permeable body, 
hands white as snow.

Half here, half there
mingling with spirits and men.
She makes humming birds her friends

Nervous yet amused,
defiant but afraid.
invisible at will


She chose to 
She knows how to make herself visible
yet she choses not to.

Half here, half there
mingling with spirits and men.
She makes humming birds her friends

Sometimes she tells herself
that she prefers it that way
even though that's not 
completely true

There are times she wants to shine
to be seen in full glory
But she fears it too.
She has seen what happens 
when you are in the eye of the hurricane
Praise and blame, coming in equal quantity
so much love and hate seems unbearable

so she navigates 
half here, half there
Mingling with spirits and men
making humming birds her friends
But if you are ever lucky enough
to enter her grounds at sundown
you may catch her bathing in the last sun's rays.

Her fingers sparkling with golden light
her eyes, sending a prayer to the pink sky
amused by the fluffy clouds so that her laugh
resonates through the trees, lingering in the night

A fairy, a goddess, a muse.
An image you won't forget
like a vivid dream
of the divine.
