Quan Yin, 

hear my call,

witness my dance.



Brief moment 

in which solid ground, 

becomes dust.



The ephimeral song 

that once heard

is gone.

___

I despise duality

revolve at the idea of unision

but this paradox

just offers 

ten thousand joys 

and ten thousand sorrows

for unity to be found.

Quan Yin, hear my call

witness my dance.


That brief moment 
in which what we thought 
solid ground, becomes
air, dust, sand.


The ephimeral song 

that once its heard

is gone.

The ephimeral song 

that once heard

is gone.

Ten thousand joys,

and ten thousand sorrows.

____

unpleasantness is the key
to connection

that brief moment 
in which we realize
what we thought solid ground 
becomes
sand, dust, air.
