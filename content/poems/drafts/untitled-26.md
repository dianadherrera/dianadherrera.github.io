_paraphrased from a dharma talk given by Gil Fronsdal_

fool: one who harms itself and others.
anatta: you are what you do, only for that moment.

If you are a fool
may you become wise
in this next moment.


____

wholesome / unwholesome
healthy / unhealthy


selfhood: the process of becoming into a self
two kinds that we can take up: wholesome or unwholesome


it turns out we don't have to hold on to our identity

unless it is a functionable use and therefore something temporary (impermanent)
