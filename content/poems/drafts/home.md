The smell of boiled corn

makes my head turn.

A smoky pot 

load with precious gold.



A wooden stick in one hand,

pinch of salt and lemon on the other.

Then as craftswomen

I devote to the endeavor.



Juicy and tender bites

saturating my taste buds

my breath floats into the night

while I wonder about home.
