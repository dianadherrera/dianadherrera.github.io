A glimpse of light amidst the sky,

followed by the thunder strike.

White engraved in my eyes,

tremble in the heart.


Be lightening!

disappear mid air.

a footprint on sand,

the ripple on water.


Truth beyond trace

grace without face.


Be the dragon! 

an ungraspable thread.

Touched but not marked,

unyielding strength.


Hold your breath 

ground your feet,

recite the prayer.

No storm lasts forever.


__

A glimpse of light amidst the sky,

followed by the thunder strike.

an image engraved in my eyes,

tremble in your thighs.


The truth with no trace,

a footprint on sand,

a ripple on water.


Be impacted 

but nor marked,

transformed

but not conditioned.


Be lightening!

disappear mid air

without anchor 

nor face.


Be the dragon! 

ungraspable thread.

The reminisce of grace,

unyielding strength.


Hold your breath 

touch the ground and remember:

No storm lasts forever.


Rest your head on the ground

breathe 

and remember the storm will pass.

___

A glimpse of light amidst the dark sky,

hear the thunder strike,

the tremble in your thighs.


The truth with no trace,

like a footprint on sand,

a ripple on water.


Be impacted 

but nor marked,

transformed

but not conditioned.


Be lightening!

disappear mid air

without anchor 

nor face.


Be the dragon! 

ungraspable thread.

The reminisce of grace,

unyielding strength.


Hold your breath 

to the ground and remember

No storm 

lasts forever.


Lean your head on the ground

and remember the storm will pass.
