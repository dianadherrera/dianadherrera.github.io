I create home wherever I go.
Cheesy nicknames, loud hellos,
silly jokes in a serious tone.

l live to laugh, love to live.
I am here, I am home,
wonder is where I belong.

the moon up high
innocence of a child
strange situations
met with lightness of heart.

Lavander scent, silent pace
tipping toes, memories of grace.
closer to myself, I become.

__

I belong to wonder,

that is how I become 
closer to myself.
