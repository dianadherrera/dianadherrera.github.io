Five seconds was all it took
to decide my worth.
Your back already turned
when I reached for your love.
If only I were born a man,
would you have looked again?

__

Indecision,
killer queen of creativity.
Snipes at enthusiasm 
building up
until there's just smoke
from the gunshot.

____
