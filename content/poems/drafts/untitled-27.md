I hope my words 
don't reach you,
not even in dreams

more and more, 
my interactions with you 
seem to happen only in my imagination
only in the realm of the non-existant
the ethereal 

I just have demands on you
just wants, wishes to be fulfilled
would you be my lottery ticket?
when did I first saw you as such?
