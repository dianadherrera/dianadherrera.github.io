There is a body that aches,
rejoices and decays.

Full of sensations 
we cannot control
that arise and fade
and we ignore.

This body is nature
not different from a tree,
we water the roots
of what we want to see.

this body has wisdom
indivisible from its looks
it knows and warns;
attunement its strong suit

This body's resilience,
takes miss over fail
its forgiving unbounded
for the opening of faith. 

This body has pain
the key to break our chains
its continuous arising
an opportunity, a gate.

If held mistakenly as a burden
nothing but suffering awaits
but seen as a thread
understanding will lead the way.

______

that won't lead you astray.

taking success and mistakes





if we mistakenly see it as a burden 

or an unwanted experience

we bound ourselves for a life of suffering

thus is through mindfulness of the body

that we taste 

we wont taste the joy of delight
