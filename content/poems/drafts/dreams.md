I have dreams

that seem unnatainable

dreams

that seem unreachable

until they are


Until I decide it is time

and the pain of not fullfilling them

becomes stronger than doing nothing.  to let them pass


I crave to fly, to be free

to be unafraid

to build 

but i don't seem to know 

how to break free

everything stays in mind blueprints

so detailed and beautiful

that i just want to smash the 

glass ceiling to let them be free
