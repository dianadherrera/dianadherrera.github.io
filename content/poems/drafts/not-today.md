This is not the day I die,
or become.

Not the day to rush, 
or perform.

It is the distant plane,
that threads the clouds 
in its ascent,

which inspires me to marvel,
to be, to rest.

__

This is not the day I die,
or rush, or become.

Not the day I write,
impress, or perform.

It is the distant plane,
that threads the clouds 
in its ascend,

which reminds me to marvel
at the violet streaks
to be, to rest.

___

This is not the day I die,
or rush, or become.

This is not the day I write,
or impress, or perform.

And so, it is the distant plane,
that threads the clouds 
in its ascend,

which reminds me to marvel,
at the violet streaks
and the cicadas buzz.

To let the ink sink,
to be, to rest.

__

which reminds me to marvel,
at the streaks above—violet-pink—
and the cicadas buzz.

Today I let the ink sink
I rest,
today I "be"
and let the ink, sink.



It is the day I marvel
at the pink, purple streaks,
the fluffy straight line in the sky.

the fluffy straight line in the sky.
