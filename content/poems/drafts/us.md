Beauty,

Do you see it, too?

It's all around us.



From the lukewarm glass cup

with used up coffee grains

in the sink,



to the garden's hose

sprinkling water

over the oak tree.



I couldn't be so arrogant

as to say we've crafted this

by ourselves;



but more like we've gradually
come to surrender to life
as it has opened to us each morning.

And for that, I celebrate.

I cheer for the perseverance,
willingness and resilience
we have entrusted to,
with no guarantee 
of success.

I commend our efforts
to not submit but
integrate.

I praise our inner strength
to be vulnerable
and connect.

And while we couldn't have 
planned it this way,
let me just say:

I love our life together.

The quiet joy
our daily interactions
are imbued with.

The serene vibe
at bedtime; 
the mellow lulllabies. 

So, HAPPY ANNIVERSARY TO US!!
Written as a rambunctious 
and loud claim.

May we continue honoring
our differences,
as opportunities to grow.

May we laugh mischievously
for all our years 
to come.

__

# US

Beauty, 

Do you see it, too? 

It's all around us. 



From the lukewarm cup 

with used-up coffee grains

in the sink, 



to the garden's hose 

sprinkling water 

over the oak tree. 



I couldn't be so arrogant 

as to say we've crafted this 

all by ourselves, 



but more like we've gradually 

come to surrender 

to what has opened for us

each morning. 



And for that, I celebrate. 



I cheer for the perseverance, 

willingness and resilience 

we have entrusted to, 

with no guarantee 

of success. 



I commend our efforts 

to not submit 

but assimilate. 



Praise our inner strength 

to be vulnerable 

and connect. 



And while we couldn't have 

planned it this way, 

let me just say: 



I love our life together. 



The quiet joy 

our daily interactions 

are imbued with. 



The serene vibe 

at bedtime; 

mellow lullabies. 



So, HAPPY ANNIVERSARY TO US!! 

Written as a rambunctious 

and loud claim. 



May we continue 

honoring our differences 

as opportunities to grow. 



May we laugh mischievously 

for all our years 

to come.


___









My contracted heart
softens at the warmth
of your gaze.


not the rumbuctious 
or loud kind, but the quiet
and serene joy, of our presence.

I have been shaped
by your presence.



____

I commend our efforts
to bend and not
to break.

The quiet and serene joy
our daily interactions
are imbued with.

I adore the joy
imbued into our 
daily interactions.

I adore the joy
we've let sip
into our daily grind

we've let sip in.
