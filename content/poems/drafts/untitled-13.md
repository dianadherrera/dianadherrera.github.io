Creativity

The daunting task of shaping
specks of dust
into shimmerings of light.

Nothing but a draft, our life.
Struggling to emerge 
from imposed boundaries.

The imprint of our soul
in every word choice.

Magic spells
of unexplored meaning.

May I live
with the endless chance
to change what was written.

__

In search of more adequate words
that fully convey this paradox of existence.

__ 

that makes the creation process
so alluring

the aha moment, where 
disorganized thoughts give way
to a cohesive, not always understandable
statement of what we feel, of how we feel


the choice to pick any word
in an ocean full of options, opportunities
makes the autonomy, the freedom
of exploring even words we don't yet know
even more meaningful
that is their magic

may I live my life with the endless chance
to change what was written
in search of more adequate words
that fully convey this paradox of existence.
