Your heart sinks at the first chord 
as the violin pierces through.
Memories of butterflies 
lived in your youth.

Reality and illusion blend
as it comes to you again.
The meadow in spring,
hollyhocks dancing in the air,
her figure swirling in the red dress
the sweet smell of rain.

Reach your hand to the grass
to the freshness of days passed.
*How I wish I could go back*,
*to the softness of her eyes*.
A thought without meaning
that briefly crosses the mind.

Thus it was the innocence, 
the naivety of the flow of time,
the ignorance of closing doors
what made those moments gold.
Precious twinkles of joy
now that you are old.

___

There is no place for a man -----
filled with longing for the past
Leave it to the centaur
living in two worlds at once

Going back would tear you in two
becoming a centaur, neither here nor there. 
Grasping the past as you gallop to the future, 
gradually calling for disaster.

The image starts to break,
the stab of the arrow feels sharper.
*I am glad*, you say this time with conviction.
it is a melody of departure, but it is not sad.

We are not separate at all.
I'll see you again, in the snow, 
in the moonlight, I'll hear about you
in each folk tune and dance, 
your voice always in the waltz.

You won't fade, 
even if my hairline does. I promise.
I'll meet you one day, fully fleshed.
And we will sing this song.


______ 





I'll keep my promise
