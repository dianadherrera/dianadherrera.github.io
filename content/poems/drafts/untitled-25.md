May i find:
the strength to release the struggle

May i have:
the confidence to let go into kindness

May courage
May I bear:
forgetting precious memories

May i have the courage to release the struggle of wanting 1 instead of 2. Of wanting this but not that.
To be spacious enough to hold both. To bare both

Dame el coraje de no dejar mis palabras en el tintero

Oh god of war,
let me yield My Will.
Give me your Will, to yield My weapons
To destruct only untruths/illusions/fools/
And be the Warrior of peace.
Give me the candor to embrace the pain of fear

Give me the equanimity to embrace pain as dear
