Surely, I was the one 
being desired, 
pined for.

the one in control,
who denied, 
free to choose 
whenever I want.

Didn't notice the path I took
was your morning route.
I was the wizard of oz,
playing the fool.

a smug smile on my face.

I blocked highways, 
painted luminiscent signs,
gave directions,
so you went my way.

The crave for rejection
is how that's call now
Th panic of feeling unlovable,
to chase for your worth.
