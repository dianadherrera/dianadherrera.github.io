I remember when our eyes met,
it was love at first sight.
I bowed to the mountain
as she bowed to me.

I entered to her grounds
leaves cracking softly below my weight

The leaves cracked at my feet
as if telling me where to walk.
They fell as red smooth petals 
placed by a devoted lover
telling me where to walk
to find the path of love.

Her leaves created a path

Her leaves pattern
seemed like the path
a devoted lover would do,
placing smooth red petals
for me to find the path of love. 

Her leaves placed 
as a devoted lover
creating a path of smooth petals
for me to find love.

The leaves cracked at my feet
as if telling me where to walk.
They fell as red smooth petals 
placed by a devoted lover
telling me where to walk
to find the path of love.


From time to time 
I would hear birds singing 
or bushes rustling 
but mostly
I heard only silence.

as if protecting me 
of the envious gossiping
of the trees.

when I arrived at the peak
You had already leave me 
breathless

but as I stand still
I could hear you whisper,
I love you.


___

blew me a kiss from afar.
