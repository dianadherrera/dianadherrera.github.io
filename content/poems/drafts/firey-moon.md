I've been hurt but there is no wound

my mind wretches my heart at will,

using words as knives.


Not enough love, much sorrow


what is there to do when you are envious?

Go talk to the moon tonight

I can no longer hold its fire.



Just a rewatch


who is trying to understand it?
who is trying to embrace it?



it hurts to be in the absence of love

___

when there are not enough words?

what happens when there is not enough love 

and much sorrow?
