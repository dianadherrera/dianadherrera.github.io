"Someday I'll miss..."
started the poem.

Innocent words, 
protected
by the future tense.

How often
does someday become today?

The longing
for days outside time,

Wonder 
within the reach of my hand.

Those nights,
I never imagined myself
without.




___

How distant
the statement 
read.

How distant in that moment
does someday seemed.

when days stretched
when wonder was
at the reach of my hand.

I didn't even have time
to mourn for those quiet times

Easily taken as another's life
another life, 
nothing to have nada que ver conmigo

Of days stretched,
time bending around them.

Precious and fleeting
were that presence, 
the deep stillness.


Fleeting presence 
of precious moments

Fleeting moments 
of grace.

presence.
