The daunting task of shaping
specks of dust
into shimmerings of light.

Struggling to emerge 
from imposed boundaries.
Nothing but a draft, our life.

The imprint of our soul
in every word choice.

Magic spells
of unexplored meaning.

May I live
with the endless chance
to change what was written.
